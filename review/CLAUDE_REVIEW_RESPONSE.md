**Accepted as a major issue.** The paper now distinguishes the true admissible set from the estimated admissible set, introduces admissible/inadmissible/indeterminate outcomes, conservative confidence bounds, false admissibility, gate regret, and a dedicated falsification requirement.

## 2. Formalism before evidence

**Accepted.** Core sections now display evidence-status notices. Lie, Grassmann, Koopman, Euler-Poincare, Lyapunov, and SFMDS transfers are explicitly deferred hypotheses. The package remains an internal hypothesis catalogue, not a committed architecture.

## 3. Route-function alignment estimator

**Accepted.** Three estimators are specified: intervention/SVD with principal angles, Jacobian subspace alignment, and counterfactual contribution correlation. Repair requires diagnostic agreement rather than one geometric score.

## 4. Repetitive twelve-year-old sections

**Accepted.** The main explanatory section remains, but reference-specific child-level paraphrases are trimmed to the conceptual anchors. Later references retain engineering explanations only.

This update is an internal freeze for context retention. It is not a peer-review rebuttal claiming closure through evidence. The conceptual issues are addressed in the specification; empirical closure remains pending under the private release-gate plan.
